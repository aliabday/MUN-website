Agenda

Implementation of Hindi as the National Language


The question of adopting Hindi as the National Language of India has been debated for a long time now. In this simulation of the AIPPM, delegates will discuss the need for a national language.




The Executive Board

Sanya Puri

Chairperson

Dear members of the AIPPM,


My name is Sanya Puri and I am honoured to be the moderator of the All India Political Parties Meet (AIPPM). I recently gave my class 12th CBSE board exams. I have been involved in Model UN since I was in school. I attended my first conference in grade 8. MUNs have enabled me to hone a wide variety of skills ranging from communication to critical thinking. To facilitate a high-quality debate, it is necessary that you are thoroughly versed with the portfolios of the individuals and the parties that you represent. Remember that you may face severe opposition by other representatives for various arguments and decisions that you make, but it is your ability to be able to stand your ground that counts. While addressing the issue at hand, I expect a certain level of decorum in the committee. Lastly, I encourage you all to utilize as much original thinking as possible. I hope you all enjoy these three days!


Wish you all the very best.

Nandini Chaudhry

Vice-Chairperson

Greetings delegates,


"All autobiographies are lies"; so said the great playwright G.B Shaw , yet here I am trying to present myself to you in the most objective and honest avatar as I possibly can. I have often been described as an inquisitive mind having a plethora of interests. Apart from MUNing which I am extremely passionate about and have actively been participating since 2019, I have a great interest in history (yes, I did want to become an archaeologist for the longest time) with a slight bias towards medieval India. If not MUNing you will find me in the drama room frantically trying to organise a play or two. I have an unending love story with theatre. In appreciation of my ability as a theatre artist I have been fetted in various foras. You will rarely ever find me separated from my headphones which is something extremely dear to my heart. With a slightly concerning obsession with consuming true crime content in any form whether it be reading books, watching documentaries or listening to podcasts. That a particular interest in debating across varied platforms, and the need to see justice delivered, has contributed a siginificant amount in my gradual love for law over the recent years. 10 years down the line I hope to be making a difference to the lives of deserving undertrials through my legal practice. You can always approach me whether it is to seek advice regarding school, have animated rants about your favourite celebrity or discuss the causes and solutions of the European migrant crisis! All of this is to say meet Nandini Chaudhry, just another 11th grader and also your vice chair for the All Indian Political Party Meet for the Interschool Step by Step MUN 2022.


Above all, I hope that you enjoy the committee and have an engaging time at the SBSMUN2019.

Shivom Singh

Rapporteur

My name is Shivom Singh, and I have been granted the privilege of serving as your rapporteur for the All India Political Parties Meet at SBSMUN 2022. I am currently in grade 10 pursuing IGCSE with greater focus towards commerce and humanities. Apart from MUN, I have varied interests, I am deeply invested in politics, poetry and dramatics. I am an ardent supporter of Liverpool Football Club (and Kanye West, but we won’t talk about that). I have been pursuing MUN since I was 11, so I’d like to think that I am somewhat experienced. I began my journey as a delegate who spoke in favour of the wrong party and was berated by the EB for a good half hour, so, it feels rather full circle, to be sitting on the chair of the EB today (hopefully not berating one of you, though). MUN to me is more than the certificates or gavels you will win. Now, this will sound extremely cliche, but ,MUN is truly about friendship, learning and most importantly understanding different perspectives. I hope to aid you all in your experience of MUNning and make this MUN a memorable one.
