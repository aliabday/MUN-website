CIA Cabinet Crisis Committee

Agenda

Discussion on Allende’s Socialist Measures in 1973


Salvador Guillermo Allende Gossens, the 28th President of Chile has introduced measures to nationalize industries, expand education and improve living conditions through government interventions. The United States of America is alarmed by these developments and the CIA is keeping a close watch on the situation




The Executive Board

Sanya Puri

Chairperson

Dear members of the AIPPM,


My name is Sanya Puri and I am honoured to be the moderator of the All India Political Parties Meet (AIPPM). I recently gave my class 12th CBSE board exams. I have been involved in Model UN since I was in school. I attended my first conference in grade 8. MUNs have enabled me to hone a wide variety of skills ranging from communication to critical thinking. To facilitate a high-quality debate, it is necessary that you are thoroughly versed with the portfolios of the individuals and the parties that you represent. Remember that you may face severe opposition by other representatives for various arguments and decisions that you make, but it is your ability to be able to stand your ground that counts. While addressing the issue at hand, I expect a certain level of decorum in the committee. Lastly, I encourage you all to utilize as much original thinking as possible. I hope you all enjoy these three days!


Wish you all the very best.

Anoushka Puljal

Vice-Chairperson

Greetings delegates,


The name's Puljal, Anoushka Puljal, and I shall be serving as your vice chair in simulation of the CIA Crisis Cabinet at the Step By Step School Inter MUN, 2022. We shall be taken back in time to the 1973 Coup d' Etat in Chile, and shall possibly be the ones masterminding it (who knows). I am currently studying in grade 11 in the IB curriculum with Biology, Chemistry and English Literature at Higher Level and History, French and Mathematics at Standard Level. Interestingly, though my passion is very science oriented (I aspire to be a veterinarian), as can be clearly inferred from my subject choices, I've always had a soft spot for history and politics, and I find MUN is the perfect amalgamation of that. That when induced into a crisis committee creates a wonderland for me where I can debate, research and imaginate in balance. Crisis committees are therefore nuanced in a way no other committee can be as it stimulates both the creative and logical sides of your brain. Through this simulation of the crisis committee, I look forward to a session filled with fervent questioning, substantial research, quick thinking and resolution building and finally, well articulated debate. "I don't expect people to just sit there and agree with me," as Margret Thatcher said, and I agree with her. I hope that everyone comes to the committee with their own individual opinions and ideas on how to tackle the issue of Allende's socialist government.


Above all, I hope that you enjoy the committee and have an engaging time at the SBSMUN2019.
