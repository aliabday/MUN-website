Registration Process

Eligibility Criteria

Grades IX-XII

(Ages 14-18)

                     

Venue

Step By Step School,

Plot A-10, Taj Expressway, Sector-132,

Noida 201303

Uttar Pradesh, India

                     

Conference Fee

Rs. 3000 per participant

*There is no participant fees for members of delegations from Round Square schools.

                     

School Delegations

o School delegations must fill the online form:

o Further details as well as a link for registration are also available on the SBSMUN 2022 website http://2022.sbsmun.in.

o A school delegation can have 5-8 members.

o The school delegation award is based on the performance of delegates alone.

o There is a separate award for members of the International Press.

o Schools willing to send more than one delegation are required to fill up a separate registration form and specify that it is a separate delegation.

o Members of the International Press will be assigned teams which may include members from different schools.

o Each delegation may be accompanied by ONE Faculty Adviser on each day of the conference. There is no registration fees for the Faculty Adviser.

o Delegate fees is INR 3000 per delegate/ member of International Press.

     - There is no separate school delegation fee.

     - Payment details will be sent to the Faculty Adviser and Head Delegate after receiving the online registration form.

     - A school delegation may make a collective payment, or each member of the school delegation can make an individual payment via electronic transfer or send a cheque.

     - Proof of payment should be compiled by the Head Delegate and sent to SBS in the format which will be made available to you post registration.

     - Apart from this, a file containing screenshots of fund transfer/ cheque should also be provided.

     - Portfolios will be assigned after payment has been received.

o The Secretariat will decide the final list of allotments.

                     

Individual Delegate/ Member of International Press

o Individual delegates may apply to any of the committees.

o Members of International Press can apply as journalist and cartoonist. To be considered for an award, members will be expected to make a pre-conference submission.

o Individual participants must produce a scan/ photocopy of a valid school ID card before their final registration and must carry the same on the two days of the conference.

o Payment details will be sent after receiving the online registration form.

o The portfolio will be assigned after payment has been received.

                     

Last Date of Registration: 13th October 2022

                     

o School Delegation Form: https://tinyurl.com/SBSMUN2022-Delegation-Form

o Individual Delegate/ International Press Form: https://tinyurl.com/SBSMUN2022-Individual

                     

Dress Code

o Business formals or Indian formals are the only accepted forms of attire.

o Casual wear will not be condoned.
