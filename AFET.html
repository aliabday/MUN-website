Agenda

The Chinese Investment in Europe Under BRI


In light of the Ukrainian War, Chinese investment in the Belt and Border Initiative in European countries have reduced. The plan to link China and the markets of the East with that of the West, giving rise to a new-age silk route seems to have been halted as China has diverted its focus towards the Middle East, specifically Saudi Arabia. This will impact Europe and its role in transcontinental trade.
